# Presenter check

Before the session, the presenter should be able to answer these questions without opening source code:

- What is Azazel-Edge?
- Why deterministic decision support?
- Why split NOC and SOC evaluation?
- What actions can the arbiter select?
- What evidence was used?
- Why was the selected action chosen?
- Why were other actions rejected?
- Where is the audit trail?
- What role does AI play?
- Why does the demo use replay?
- What happens in normal live operation?
- What is the fallback if Web UI, Suricata, OpenCanary, AI, or network access fails?
